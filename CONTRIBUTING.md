### Step 3: Verification and Review

#### üë• Review Team
Your application will be reviewed by:
- **Technical Lead**: Evaluates technical qualifications and contribution plans
- **Medical Advisor**: Reviews healthcare background and clinical contributions (if applicable)
- **Legal Team**: Ensures compliance documentation is complete
- **Project Manager**: Coordinates overall approval process

#### ‚è±Ô∏è Review Timeline
- **Initial Review**: 3-5 business days after document submission
- **Technical Interview** (if required): Scheduled within 1 week
- **Final Decision**: Within 10 business days of complete application
- **Total Process Time**: Typically 2-3 weeks from initial submission

#### üîç Review Criteria
We evaluate contributors based on:

**Technical Qualifications**:
- Relevant technical skills and experience
- Quality of previous open source contributions
- Understanding of healthcare technology requirements
- Commitment to quality and best practices

**Healthcare Alignment**:
- Understanding of medical ethics and patient safety
- Familiarity with healthcare regulations and compliance
- Appropriate medical background (for clinical contributions)
- Commitment to evidence-based healthcare practices

**Collaboration Fit**:
- Alignment with project values and mission
- Ability to work effectively in distributed teams
- Communication skills and professionalism
- Long-term commitment to the project

- Code review and feedback
- Healthcare domain knowledge sharing
- Career development support

#### üéØ First Contribution Guidelines
Start with these types of contributions:
- Documentation improvements
- Test coverage enhancements
- Bug fixes in non-critical areas
- UI/UX improvements
- Translation/localization work

**Avoid these for first contributions**:
- Critical clinical algorithms
- Security-sensitive code
- Database schema changes
- Complex medical decision logic
